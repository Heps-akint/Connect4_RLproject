How to Play

Connect 4 is a two-player game where the goal is to connect four of your colored discs in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

You'll play against an AI trained using a neural network and Monte Carlo Tree Search.

Click on any column to drop your piece. The AI will respond with its move.

First to connect 4 in a row wins!
